Private customers

Safe offers its private customers Finland's best and most comprehensive insurance services - insure yourself today!

Safe has a long history as a Finnish insurance company. From our services you will find accident, home, travel insurances and much more. We aim to offer the cheapest possible insurances especially for students, so the new phase in life would be as secured as possible. By insuring from us, you'll join a group of thousands of satisfied customers.

More detailed information about our services can be found from our for students and for seniors pages, where we more specifically explain the details of the insurances. By ordering our newsletter you will receive up to date information about the newest deals and services. If you didn't find an answer to your question you can also be in contact with us, we aim to get back to you during 24 hours.
