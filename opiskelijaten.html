Insurances for students

Student's home insurance

You can choose from up to three packages to find the best fit for you.

Insurance package S fits for those who want the minimal price for their insurance. Our cheapest package includes responsibility insurance, which most land lords require from their tenants.

Insurance package M fits for those who want more comprehensive security for their personal property. M-package covers theft insurance, which covers the theft of personal property from inside your home. Breaking into student apartments is quite common which is why one should prepare for it by insuring.

Insurance package L is our most comprehensive package, it fits for those who want as much security on their personal property as possible. A student's economy can sometimes be tight, but when it comes to home insurance, one should not attempt to haggle.

Student's accident insurance

Four reasons to take accident insurance

1. An accident doesn't happen to your wallet, as the insurances covers healthcare even in private institutions.

2. A surprising accident can happen at home, work, school or in hobbies. You can expand your insurance to cover sport accidents as well.

3. You can expand your accident insurance with our comprehensive accident. All injuries do not justify compensation, even if they come forward in context of said accident. With the expanded insurance we offer additional compensation for the moving and the sporty.

4. You can additionally insure your short term incapability to work, or permanent health damage from an accident. You can insure your close family's health and in the case of injuries or death.

Student's travel insurance

Remember to pack your insurance with you when traveling, with travel insurance you cover yourself and damage to your personal property, which makes traveling a little more stress free. Continuous travel insurance can never be forgotten, and is always ready to go when you are. It is the easiest and cheapest option during both in-country and abroad traveling.

Student's life insurance

Life insurance covers the insured persons death. Voluntary life insurances completes the legally bound social insurance. Although Finland's social insurance is quite comprehensive, it's benefits defining is schematic and is based on the average citizen's needs. People do however have varying living situations and needs for security, which are completed by private insurances.
